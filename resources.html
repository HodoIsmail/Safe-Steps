Resources

Training
Safeguarding adults and children training courses

Classroom, tailored and e-learning safeguarding training courses. Supporting you to make sure that safeguarding measures are in place to safeguard adults and children from harm or neglect.

Safeguarding consultancy, reviews and audits

Expert support to review and learn from safeguarding incidents including statutory case reviews, routine audits and learning reviews across children's and adults' services.

Safeguarding Adults in Sport and Activity Advice & Training

Safeguarding adults is a responsibility for every sport and physical activity organisation. Getting safeguarding right will ensure a wider participation in sport or physical activity and ensure safe access for everyone.

Safeguarding training for schools, academies and colleges

Online child protection course for anyone working in education Improve your understanding and confidence in recognising and responding to concerns about a child with our interactive elearning course from the safeguarding experts.

Useful Links
There are numerous resources available for safeguarding, including websites and helplines. Below are a few links that provide in-depth insights.
Websites

These websites provide information and guidance on safeguarding:

  • Think U Know: Information from CEOP about online abuse and exploitation
  • Childnet: Information for parents and carers
  • NSPCC Online Safety: Advice on online safety
  • NSPCC Pants: Helps children understand that their body belongs to them
  • NSPCC Net Aware: Information and guides for parents and carers about social media platforms, apps, and games

Helplines

These helplines can offer support:

  • National Association for People Abused in Childhood: 0808 801 0331 or E: support@napac.org.uk
  • GamCare: 0203 092 6964
  • ChildLine: Free helpline for children and young people in the UK
  • National Bullying Helpline: 0808 80 10 800
